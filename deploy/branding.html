Republic Bank Rebrand

Coingo provides loans and commercial banking services for small businesses, families, and individuals so they can protect the customer’s money from theft. Unlike competitors, Coingo aims to have the best customer service with a limited number of in-person interactions, living up to their motto of “devoted to you”. Originally called Republic bank I changed the name to Coingo for a full rebrand as their past name had 2 other banking services with the same or similar name. In addition, they had bad reviews and media so it made more sense to give a full rebrand including the name change. The name was chosen because it appeals more to a younger audience, one of the key elements for the rebrand. The new target audience was people my age and I surveyed my peers and coingo was the most desired name for their banking services. This rebrand consists of a logo change, stationary, website, atm, bank card, poster, billboard concepts, social media, exterior branding, and a few merchandise options. Ron Jon Surf Shop Rebrand

Ron Jon Surf Shop is a company that is known for selling surf, beach, and active lifestyle products to people all across the globe. Most of their locations can be found at the beach, making their locations a token beach vacation spot. In the rebrand, I focused on the logo and making sure that their new logo fit within their brand identity. In addition, there are a few brand extension applications such as the van and the gif. The logo with the surfboard is the primary logo that was used for the extension of the brand. The other two logos were secondary alternatives.
