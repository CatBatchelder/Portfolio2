About the Designer

Catherine or more commonly known as Cat to her peers is a graphic design major at Drexel University. With a dual minor in fine arts and marketing, she is well rounded for the industry. Born and raised in Annapolis, MD, and currently centered in Philadelphia holds the gates to the creative mind.

MY STORY

The story of getting to Graphic Design is quite a unique journey. My first creative passion was dance, I danced for over 12 years growing up, until. I had a bad accident and had to stop. Needing a way to let out all the creative ideas flowing in my head I had to take art classes at my high school. Soon realizing that there is no money being a painter for the rest of my life I looked into what other paths I could take with a creative feild. I soon found graphic design and took a summer class at Maryland Institute College of Art and absolutly fell in love with the craft. I would constantly find myself in the studio for hours and hours past when everyone else left. Thats when I found out graphic design is my passion.
