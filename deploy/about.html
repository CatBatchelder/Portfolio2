About the Designer

Catherine or more commonly known as Cat to her peers is a graphic design major at Drexel University. With a minor in marketing, she is well rounded for the industry. Born and raised in Annapolis, MD, and currently centered in Philadelphia, she holds the gates to the creative mind.

MY STORY

My story of getting to Graphic Design is quite a unique journey. My first creative passion was dance; I danced competitively for over 12 years growing up until I had a bad accident and had to stop. Needing a way to let out all the creative ideas flowing in my head, I took art classes at my high school. I began looking into what career paths I could take within a creative field. I soon found graphic design and took a summer class at Maryland Institute College of Art and fell in love with the craft. I would constantly find myself in the studio for hours and hours past when everyone left. That's when I found out graphic design is my passion. Flash forward four years, I have my B.S. in graphic design and am still eager to learn more about the beautiful craft.
